Recipe Variations

  1. step 1
    Fudgy and chocolatey: Top a rich, dense brownie bottom with a light, moist butter cake to make a Brownie Butter Cake that’s irresistible.
  2. Nutty: Add ground walnuts to the batter and sprinkle some whole ones on top for a delicious Walnut Butter Cake.
  3. Raisins: For a sweet twist, load up your cake with raisins in this Raisin Cake recipe.
  4. Blueberry: Bake a Blueberry Butter Cake (or Blueberry Cake) and enjoy a burst of color and flavor in every bite.
  5. Brandy-flavored with prunes: Try a Brandy Butter Cake with Prunes, adding a splash of brandy and dried prunes for a unique, flavorful twist.
